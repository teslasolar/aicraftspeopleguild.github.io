Tsvetan Tsvetanov

Chief Context Officer

I can help your team ship production-grade software with AI. Without drowning in code reviews or debugging AI slop.

With 12 years as a professional software engineer and lead, Tsvetan has spent his career learning what makes systems hold up under real-world use and how those same principles must be applied to AI-assisted development.

In his view, most teams fall into one of two traps: they are either skeptical of AI tooling altogether, or they use it to generate loads of unmaintainable code and bury themselves in review and cleanup.

Standing on the shoulders of giants, he created the DeSlopify Method to address that failure mode. It is an engineering approach built on three pillars:

  • Behavior-Driven Development - executable specifications that guide AI instead of vague prompts.
  • Small Steps - fast feedback loops that prevent rabbit holes.
  • Hexagonal Architecture - high-level verification of AI output without reviewing thousands of lines in detail.

He runs one-day onsite workshops for small engineering teams that want hands-on practice building confidence to ship AI-assisted code sustainably. No inflated productivity theater. No "10x" mythology. Just practical work.

His recent webinar attracted more than 100 engineers and leaders looking for a better way, and the first paid workshop cohort was already drawing strong interest for December.

If a team is struggling with AI slop or sitting on the sidelines while competitors move faster, his work is aimed squarely at that gap.

Tsvetan is also part of Camplight, where the core members are owners and entrepreneurs who still stay deep in the technical work. The result is a business-oriented engineering culture without losing engineering depth.
